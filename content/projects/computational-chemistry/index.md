I use molecular dynamics, docking, Markov state modeling, pathway analysis, and allosteric network analysis to study how molecular perturbations reshape protein conformational states. This direction grew from work on SauCas9 inhibition by AcrIIA14, SpCas9 catalytic activation, and allosteric knowledgebase construction.
