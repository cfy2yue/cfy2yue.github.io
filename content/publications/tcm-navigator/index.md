**Citation.** **Feiying Chen**, Victor Jun Yu Lim, Mingyu Li, and Hao Fan. TCM-Navigator: a deep learning-based workflow for generation and evaluation of traditional Chinese medicine-like compounds for drug development. *Briefings in Bioinformatics*, 2025.

This work connects molecular generation with practical drug-development filters: a TCM-like chemical language model generates large focused libraries, an AttentiveFP-based identifier scores TCM-like characteristics, and physics-based evaluation helps prioritize target-ligand pairs.
